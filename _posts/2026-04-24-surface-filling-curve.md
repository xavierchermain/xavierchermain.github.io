*Field-Aligned Surface-Filling Curve via Implicit Stitching*

In this work, we present a robust and scalable method for generating field-aligned surface-filling curves on general manifolds. By stitching directly in the implicit domain, our approach is both efficient and robust, with strong alignment to the input direction field.

Project page: [link](https://xavierchermain.github.io/publications/surface-filling-curve)

Authors: [Giovanni Cocco](https://github.com/iota97) and Xavier Chermain

[Université de Lorraine](https://www.univ-lorraine.fr/), [CNRS](https://www.cnrs.fr/en), [Inria](https://inria.fr/en), [LORIA](https://www.loria.fr/en/) (Nancy, France)
